Our Game

Welcome to Jurassic Crimson!

Here at Jurassic Crimson, we plan on making your experience as good as possible. The game is currently still under development, however, we plan on releasing hopefully some time before June 2026, or even a test game.

Our staff have been working endless amount of hours working on this game for anyone who enjoys the Jurassic Park genre. Jurassic Crimson is a roleplay game and will have 2 primary teams, Humans and Dinosaurs. In the future, we will be adding newer teams to the game. Right now we plan on adding a team called Crimson Containment Unit (CCU).

We hope you enjoy your stay here at JC, and we look forward to seeing you in the present and future.

Key Features

Dinosaurs

Become a dinosaur, and play as over 30 dinosaurs in our game!. Cause terror among the humans, and rule the world, or become a friend, and help the humans throughout their journey.

Multiplayer

Our game is a multiplayer based game, and you can have up to 25 people in your server! Play with others and explore the world

Humans

Become a human, and protect yourself, others, and the base from dinosaurs that lurk.
