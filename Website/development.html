Devlog 1

The Beginning

Jurassic Crimson was found some time in January 2025. Development on the map began some time in April. We had multiple issues throughout the year, and we finally made a map that would work.

Devlog 2

First Model

Our first modelled dinosaur was send on September 29th 2025. This was the Velociraptor. After, the Allosaurus, Baryonyx, Pyrosaurus, and Microceratus were finished!
